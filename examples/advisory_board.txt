# Convergent chains — academic advisory board
# Two independent chains (publication prestige + citation count) both reach the same
# intermediate conclusion, which then triggers the final outcome.
# Premises use natural academic/professional language.
#
# Run: python scripts/demo.py --file examples/advisory_board.txt

goal: Dr. Osei will be invited to join the advisory board.

premise: Researchers who publish in a top-tier journal within their first three years are considered rising stars in their field.
premise: Any researcher whose work is cited more than fifty times in its first year is automatically nominated by the board chair.
premise: The board chair is a current board member.
premise: Rising stars who are nominated by a current board member are invited to join the advisory board.
premise: Dr. Osei published in Nature Methods in her second year.
premise: Nature Methods is a top-tier journal.
premise: Dr. Osei's paper received 73 citations in its first year of publication.
