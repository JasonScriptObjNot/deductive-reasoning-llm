# Modus tollens — fire safety inspection
# Tests reasoning by contrapositive from a universal requirement.
# Short and clean: the goal is a negation derived from a missing necessary condition.
#
# Run: python scripts/demo.py --file examples/building_inspection.txt

goal: The Harlow Building did not pass its fire safety inspection.

premise: Every building that passes a fire safety inspection has functioning sprinklers on every floor.
premise: The Harlow Building has had a non-functional sprinkler system on the third floor for six months.
